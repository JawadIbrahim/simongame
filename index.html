How to Play:

1) First a random button will blink, you need to click on it.
2) Then another random button will blink. But now you have to click on the 1st blinked button first and then click on the 2nd blinked button.
3) Again another button will blink, again you have to click serially from first to last like 1st, 2nd and 3rd blinked buttons........that means you have to maintain the serial of blinked buttons.
This will continue until your pattern is incorrect. It is like a brain game, here you have to remember the pattern serially.
Hope you will enjoy the game....
